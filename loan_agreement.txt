PERSONAL LOAN AGREEMENT
Date: 29th March 2026
Loan Agreement No: LA-2026-001
PARTIES:

Lender: Ramesh Kumar Sharma, residing at 45, MG Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka - 560001
Borrower: Priya Mehta, residing at 12, Linking Road, Mumbai, Maharashtra - 400050


CLAUSE 1 — LOAN AMOUNT
The Lender agrees to lend a sum of Rs. 5,00,000 (Five Lakhs Rupees Only) to the Borrower on the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement.
CLAUSE 2 — INTEREST RATE
The Borrower shall pay interest at the rate of 24% per annum on the outstanding loan amount. Interest shall be compounded monthly without any grace period.
CLAUSE 3 — REPAYMENT TERMS
The Borrower shall repay the loan in 12 equal monthly instalments of Rs. 47,500 each, commencing from 1st May 2026. Failure to pay any instalment within 3 days of the due date shall attract a penalty of Rs. 2,000 per day.
CLAUSE 4 — COLLATERAL
The Borrower agrees to pledge their residential property located at 12, Linking Road, Mumbai as collateral security. The Lender shall have the absolute right to seize and sell the said property without prior notice in the event of default.
CLAUSE 5 — PREPAYMENT PENALTY
In the event the Borrower wishes to repay the loan before the stipulated period, a prepayment penalty of 5% of the outstanding principal amount shall be charged.
CLAUSE 6 — ARBITRATION
Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved by arbitration in Bengaluru, Karnataka, under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The Lender shall have the sole right to appoint the arbitrator.
CLAUSE 7 — GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the Indian Contract Act, 1872 and the laws of the State of Karnataka.
CLAUSE 8 — DEFAULT
In the event of default, the Lender shall have the right to recover the entire outstanding amount along with 36% per annum interest and all legal costs from the Borrower. The Borrower waives all rights to receive prior notice before legal action is initiated.
CLAUSE 9 — CONFIDENTIALITY
The Borrower shall not disclose the terms of this Agreement to any third party. Violation of this clause shall result in a penalty of Rs. 1,00,000.
CLAUSE 10 — AMENDMENT
This Agreement may be amended unilaterally by the Lender at any time without prior notice to the Borrower. The amended terms shall be binding on the Borrower immediately upon amendment.
